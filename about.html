About Project Serenity

Project Serenity is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to support individuals through emotional awareness and intelligent conversation. Our goal is to create a safe, responsive, and empathetic space where users can express themselves freely and receive personalized support.

What We Do

Serenity combines advanced AI technologies—such as natural language processing, emotion recognition, and multimodal input analysis—to detect user emotions and offer real-time responses. The system can understand user feelings through both text and voice, offering emotional validation, motivational support, or suggestions for professional help when needed.

Core Features

  • Emotion-Aware Chatbot: Understands emotional cues in user input and responds appropriately.
  • Multimodal Emotion Detection: Analyzes both facial expressions and voice tone to determine the user’s emotional state.
  • Smart Interaction Logging: Securely stores chat logs with detected emotions to help users and professionals track emotional trends over time.
  • Mental Health Support: Offers gentle encouragement, coping strategies, or directs users to seek professional therapy based on severity.

Our Mission

We believe emotional well-being is just as important as physical health. Project Serenity aims to democratize access to emotional support through AI, making mental health care more proactive, personalized, and always available.
